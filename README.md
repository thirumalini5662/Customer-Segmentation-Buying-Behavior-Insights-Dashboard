# Customer-Segmentation-Buying-Behavior-Insights-Dashboard

This Tableau project analyzes customer demographics, purchasing behavior, spending patterns, and product preferences to identify valuable customer segments and generate actionable business insights.

The dashboard helps businesses understand customer behavior, improve marketing strategies, identify high-value customers, and optimize customer engagement efforts.

Objectives

- Segment customers based on purchasing behavior
- Identify High-Value, Frequent Buyer, and At-Risk customer groups
- Analyze category preferences and spending patterns
- Measure customer engagement through repeat purchase behavior
- Support data-driven marketing and business decisions

Key Metrics

- Total Customers
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Repeat Purchase Rate

Dashboard Components

KPI Cards

- Total Sales
